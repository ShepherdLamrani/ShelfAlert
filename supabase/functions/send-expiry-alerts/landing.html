Features

Everything you need

A focused toolkit that gives small business owners full visibility over their stock — without the complexity of enterprise software.

Colour-coded expiry alerts

Red, amber, and green status at a glance. Instantly know what's expired, expiring soon, or safe.

Daily email alerts

Automatic summaries land in your inbox every morning. Know what needs attention before you open.

Low stock warnings

Set your own thresholds. ShelfAlert flags items running low before you hit zero.

Supplier management

Store supplier contacts with one-tap WhatsApp and call buttons built right in.

Action Centre

A dedicated panel surfaces every urgent item — expired or critically low — in one place.

CSV export

Download your inventory any time for audits, compliance checks, or sharing with your team.
